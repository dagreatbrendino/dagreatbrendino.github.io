I was born and raised on the south side of Chicago. I've lived all over the city and have enjoyed the endless variety of all its neighborhoods. Even though I've always lived in Chicago, I am still able to find new and exciting things to do!

I currently work as a full stack web developer for Deep Sounds. We are a music licensing platform that aims to provide high quality music for a wide range of content while providing the best experience possible for our artists.

I formerly worked as a community organizer for People's Action, a non-profit that is dedicated to fighting for racial, economic, and environmental justice.

In 2019 I began a journey to becoming a full stack web developer by enrolling in the coding bootcamp at Northwestern. I am passionate about creative problem solving and am always looking to learn more and continue to develop my skills.
